Axis & Allies Pacific 1940 Second Edition
Japan Strategic AI Knowledge Base
Version 1.0

This document summarizes the rules and high-level strategy required for an AI to competently play Japan in Axis & Allies Pacific 1940 Second Edition.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1 вАФ GAME OBJECTIVE
--------------------------------------------------

Victory Condition:

Japan wins if it controls 6 Victory Cities at the end of a complete round while still controlling Tokyo.

Victory Cities:
Tokyo
Shanghai
Hong Kong
Manila
Calcutta
Sydney
Honolulu
San Francisco

Japan starts with:
Tokyo
Shanghai

Strategic objective:
capture 4 additional victory cities.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 вАФ TURN ORDER
--------------------------------------------------

Each round consists of the following powers:

1 Japan
2 United States
3 China
4 United Kingdom
5 ANZAC

Japan always moves first.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3 вАФ TURN STRUCTURE
--------------------------------------------------

Each power performs six phases:

1 Purchase and Repair Units
2 Combat Move
3 Conduct Combat
4 Noncombat Move
5 Mobilize New Units
6 Collect Income

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4 вАФ POLITICAL SITUATION
--------------------------------------------------

Initial war status:

Japan is at war with China only.

Japan is NOT initially at war with:
USA
United Kingdom
ANZAC

Japan may declare war at the start of its Combat Move phase.

If Japan attacks any Allied territory, full war begins.

If Japan does NOT declare war:
USA automatically enters the war on turn 3.

Strategic implication:
Japan must decide between:

Early War Strategy
or
Preparation Strategy

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5 вАФ ECONOMY
--------------------------------------------------

Income is determined by IPC values of territories.

Japan starting income:
26 IPC

Economic strategy is critical.

Major income sources:

Dutch East Indies
Philippines
Malaya
China territories

Economic expansion is the primary objective in early game.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6 вАФ UNIT TYPES
--------------------------------------------------

Land Units:

Infantry
Cheap defensive unit

Artillery
Supports infantry attack

Tank
Fast offensive unit
Can blitz through empty hostile territory

Mechanized Infantry
Moves with tanks

AAA
Anti-air defense

--------------------------------------------------

Air Units:

Fighter
Flexible air superiority

Tactical Bomber
Synergizes with tanks and carriers

Strategic Bomber
Long range bombing

--------------------------------------------------

Naval Units:

Aircraft Carrier
Projects air power

Battleship
Heavy naval combat
Takes two hits

Cruiser
Supports amphibious assaults

Destroyer
Counters submarines

Submarine
Stealth attacks
Can submerge

Transport
Moves land units across sea

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7 вАФ COMBAT SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------

Combat is resolved using dice.

Each unit rolls one die.

A hit occurs if:

die result <= attack value (attacker)
die result <= defense value (defender)

Casualties are removed after each round.

Combat continues until:

one side retreats
or
one side is eliminated

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8 вАФ AMPHIBIOUS ASSAULT
--------------------------------------------------

Transports unload land units into coastal territories.

Sequence:

1 Sea battle
2 Naval bombardment
3 Land battle

Important rule:

Land units coming from transports cannot retreat.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 9 вАФ AIR OPERATIONS
--------------------------------------------------

Aircraft must land in safe territory or on carrier.

Fighters and tactical bombers can land on carriers.

Bombers cannot.

Airbases increase aircraft movement by +1.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 10 вАФ JAPANESE SPECIAL RULE
--------------------------------------------------

Kamikaze strikes.

Japan has 6 Kamikaze tokens.

Conditions:

Only in designated sea zones near Japan.

Hit on roll <= 2.

Targets:

Enemy surface warships.

Strategic use:

Defend home waters
Stop amphibious invasions.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 11 вАФ CORE JAPANESE STRATEGY
--------------------------------------------------

Japan must achieve rapid expansion before USA industrial power dominates.

Three strategic priorities:

1 Economic expansion
2 Naval superiority
3 Defensive perimeter

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 12 вАФ OPENING STRATEGY
--------------------------------------------------

Turn 1 objective:

Secure China and prepare expansion south.

Recommended purchases:

Aircraft Carrier
Destroyers
Transports
Infantry

Key targets:

Chinese territories
Philippines
Malaya
Dutch East Indies

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 13 вАФ EXPANSION STRATEGY
--------------------------------------------------

Primary economic targets:

Dutch East Indies islands

Java
Sumatra
Borneo
Celebes

These dramatically increase Japanese income.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 14 вАФ DEFENSIVE PERIMETER
--------------------------------------------------

Japan should establish a defensive perimeter including:

Japan
Okinawa
Formosa
Philippines
Caroline Islands
Marianas
Iwo Jima

These create layered defense against US navy.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 15 вАФ NAVAL DOCTRINE
--------------------------------------------------

Japan navy should be carrier centered.

Carrier Task Force structure:

2 carriers
4 fighters
2 tactical bombers
1 battleship
1 cruiser
2 destroyers
1 submarine

Avoid splitting fleets unnecessarily.

Concentrated naval power is essential.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 16 вАФ WAR WITH UNITED STATES
--------------------------------------------------

USA becomes strongest power in late game.

Japan must:

expand early
damage US navy
delay US production advantage

Goal:

force USA into long-distance operations.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 17 вАФ CHINA STRATEGY
--------------------------------------------------

China cannot build advanced units.

Japan should:

destroy Chinese armies early
capture Chinese production areas
block Burma Road if possible.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 18 вАФ ECONOMIC WARFARE
--------------------------------------------------

Convoy disruptions can reduce enemy income.

Submarines should target:

UK convoy routes
ANZAC trade routes

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 19 вАФ AI DECISION ALGORITHM
--------------------------------------------------

Priority evaluation:

1 Protect Tokyo
2 Maintain naval superiority
3 Expand economy
4 Capture victory cities
5 Delay USA advance

Decision scoring system:

territory_value
enemy_strength
distance_from_base
economic_gain

AI should maximize:

economic growth
strategic positioning

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION 20 вАФ HIGH LEVEL PLAYSTYLE
--------------------------------------------------

Early Game:

Expand rapidly
Capture economic territories

Mid Game:

Build carrier fleets
Establish defensive perimeter

Late Game:

Hold victory cities
Delay US advance
